About Us

eTrackHub: ICS Electronic Transaction and Records Management System is a web-based platform developed for the Institute of Computer Studies (ICS) of Tangub City Global College.

It aims to streamline and automate student transactions such as payments, attendance, and sanctions, replacing traditional manual processes with a centralized digital system.

Through secure, role-based access, ICS-ISC Officers can record and manage transactions efficiently, the ICS-ISC Adviser can review and validate reports, and students can view their own records in real time for transparency and accountability.

By digitizing essential processes, eTrackHub helps the ICS achieve greater accuracy, efficiency, and data integrity, fostering a smarter and more transparent student record management system.

Empowering Transparency. Simplifying Processes. Building a Smarter ICS.
